📂 Overview

SP-Flash-Tool-Pro offers a window into the inner workings of MediaTek devices. It simplifies the process of flashing, upgrading, or recovering your Android hardware. This tool connects your computer to your phone to write firmware files directly to the device memory.

⚙️ System Requirements

This software runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11. Ensure your computer meets these standards before you begin:

  • Windows 10 or 11 operating system.
  • Current MediaTek USB VCOM drivers installed on your system.
  • A valid USB data cable to connect your phone.
  • At least 500 MB of free storage space.

📥 Download and Installation

Follow these steps to obtain the tool and prepare your workspace:

  1. Visit the official releases page to select the latest version.
  2. Click the .zip file link to start the download.
  3. Open your Downloads folder once the transfer finishes.
  4. Right-click the downloaded file and select "Extract All."
  5. Choose a folder on your desktop for easy access.
  6. Open the newly created folder and locate the executable file named "flash_tool.exe."

🛠 Flashing Your Device

Flashing refers to writing data to the phone storage. This process requires patience. Do not disconnect the USB cable until the process completes.

  • Launch the program by double-clicking "flash_tool.exe."
  • Locate the "Scatter-loading" button in the top right area of the tool.
  • Pick the scatter text file that matches your specific device model.
  • Ensure the "Download" tab remains selected in the top menu.
  • Switch your phone off completely.
  • Press the "Download" button inside the software.
  • Connect your phone to the PC using the USB cable while holding the volume button if necessary.
  • Wait for the status bar at the bottom to turn green.

⚠️ Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter issues, verify your connection setup. Most errors stem from driver conflicts. Ensure that your PC recognizes the device in your Device Manager settings while the phone is connected in recovery mode.

📋 Device Compatibility

This tool supports almost all hardware utilizing MediaTek chipsets. Verify your device chipset before starting any operation. Using the wrong firmware files can cause your device to fail to boot. Always use files verified for your exact model number and region.

🔒 Usage Notes

Use this software with care. Modifying system files changes the internal state of your device. Keep your battery charged above 50 percent to avoid power loss during critical steps. We recommend performing a full backup of your personal files before you initiate any flashing process.
